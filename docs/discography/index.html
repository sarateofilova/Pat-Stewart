Biography

Born in France and raised in New Zealand, Bassist, Keyboardist, Producer, Mix Engineer, Composer, and Arranger.

Pat Stewart started playing bass in bands in High School, beginning a professional career as a Jazz and Pop Musician before the age of 18. Pat then completed a BMus in Jazz Performance on Double Bass and Electric Bass, during which he found a love of composition and production that has been a constant vein throughout his Musical expression ever since.

After spending several years playing and writing in original projects (Brockaflower/The Strowlini Orchestra) and as a session player (Louis Baker/Thomas Oliver/ZoÃ« Moon/Deva Mahal), as well as Producer and Mix engineer (Funkacybin/Ryan Hendricks/Lucas Martinez Membrilla) Pat has relocated to Europe to continue study at the Koninklijk Conservatorium in Den Haag, The Netherlands.

Pat is available throughout Europe for Audio engineering, Teaching, and Performance.
